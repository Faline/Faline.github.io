About Me

I’m currently a student at Universitas Pelita Harapan (UPH), pursuing a degree in Information Systems as part of the class of 2023. While my studies focus on information systems, my true passion lies in graphic design. I’ve always been fascinated by the power of visual communication and how design can tell stories, create emotions, and bring ideas to life.

With an eye for detail and a creative mindset, I enjoy working on design projects that allow me to express my creativity, whether it's through logos, posters, or digital art. I’m always eager to learn new design techniques and stay updated with the latest trends in the design world.

I look forward to applying my skills and passion in both design and technology, and I’m excited about the opportunities that lie ahead in this field!
